My Story

I am Armenian Syrian, born in a small Armenian town in the north west part of Syria, at the border with Turkey. I have always been a good student at my school, which I represented in the Pan-Armenian litereture Olympiad in Armenia summer 2009. I am multilingual.I speak Armenian, Arabic, English, and French.
I have always been interested in Technology and programming specifically. After graduation from high school I decided to study Information Technology Engineering, which was practically new field in Syrian universities.
I started university the same year the Syrian war started, and even thought it was difficult for me, I never gave up and graduated with VERY GOOD esteem, and with an average of 75.67%
The program covered Networking, Programming, Algorithms, AI, Information Security, Information theory and many other materials.
I arrived in Canada with lots of dreams, carrying a nobel message from my country.
I came to Canada to represent what does it mean to be a Syrian, to be an Armenian.
